President Director

Michael Steven

Obtained an MBA degree in Corporate Finance & Banking from Golden Gate University, San Francisco in 1988, and a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science & Mathematics from University of Texas, Austin. Founded PT Kresna Graha Sekurindo Tbk in 1999 and has been serving as the Company’s President Director to date. Is licensed as Investment Manager Representative and Underwriter Representative bythe Financial Services Authority , and holds the Registered Financial Certificate (RFC) Certificate from the International Association of Registered Financial Consultants (IARFC) and Certified Wealth Manager (CWM) certificate from the Certified Wealth Managers’ Association (CWMA). Participated in the Executive Development Program at the School of Economics and Management session at Tsinghua University, Beijing, China in October 2012. He was awarded as The Most Analytical CEO in Indonesian Public Company in 2014, The Best CEO for Business Teamwork Development of the Year 2013 from the Anugerah Business Review, and The CEO of Innovations for three consecutive years. He is the founder of Kresna Financial Group that includes Kresna Creativentures (PT Kresna Usaha Kreatif), which is engaged in e-commerce infrastructure and enabler businesses, by assisting start-ups expand their digital and e-commerce businesses in Indonesia. His term as President Director based on the Resolution of the Extraordinary General Meeting of Shareholders on 24 November 2015 extends until 2020.
